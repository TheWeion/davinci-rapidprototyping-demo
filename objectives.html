DAVINCI - Goals

Understand

  • 1. Gain an understanding of Venus' Atmosphere.
  • 2. How has it evolved.
  • 3. Why it is different from the atmospheres of Earth and Mars.

Investigate

  • 1. Find evidence of an ocean in Venus' past.
  • 2. Assess the chemical processes at work in the lower Venusian Atmosphere.
  • 3. Catalogue findings.

Discover

  • 1. Obtain high-resolution images of the geological features of Venus.
  • 2. Assess whether Venus has plate tectonics.
  • 3. Gain better understanding on how terrestrial planets are formed.
